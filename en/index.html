About me

I'm Rocío, a general health psychologist, dedicated with deep commitment and motivation to my psychotherapeutic work for over ten years. I firmly believe that continuous training is a fundamental pillar of my practice.

I accompany people seeking a space where they can talk about what's happening to them, understand it, and find new ways of being. My work is based on attentive listening and caring for each person's subjectivity.

I understand that distress doesn't arise in isolation, but is constructed in relation to our history, our relationships, and the social and cultural contexts that shape us. Therefore, my practice integrates an analytical perspective with great sensitivity to diversity, relationships, and social aspects.

In session, I offer a safe space where you can express, explore, and process what may be affecting you, without judgment and at your own pace. A place where you can think (about yourself), put into words what's hard to name, and work through what causes distress.

I look forward to meeting you!
